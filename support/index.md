# DecoEcho Support

DecoEcho is a flashcard study app for iPhone and iPad with spaced repetition, Wikipedia autofill, translation, and text-to-speech.

## Get help

Email **decoecho.support@gmail.com** — we typically reply within 1–2 business days.

It helps to include:

- A description of what you were doing when the issue happened
- Your iPhone or iPad model and iOS version
- A screenshot if the issue is visual

## Frequently asked questions

### How does spaced repetition work?

DecoEcho uses an SM-2 style algorithm. When you say "Got it," the card is pushed further into the future. When you say "Missed it," it comes back soon. Over time, you study less but remember more.

### Is my data backed up?

If you have iCloud enabled for DecoEcho (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → DecoEcho), your cards, decks, and study progress sync to your iCloud and across your devices.

### What's included in DecoEcho Pro?

Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks:

- Wikipedia autofill for card answers
- Auto-translation across 8 languages
- Text-to-speech pronunciation
- Paste notes to automatically generate cards

### How do I share a deck with someone?

Open a deck and tap the share button. DecoEcho creates a `.decoechodeck` file you can share via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any other method. The recipient opens it in DecoEcho and gets their own editable copy.

### How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Sign in to the new device with the same Apple ID. Open DecoEcho, tap any Pro feature to bring up the paywall, and tap "Restore Purchases."

### How do I request a feature?

Email us at **decoecho.support@gmail.com** — we read every message.
